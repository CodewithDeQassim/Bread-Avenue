About Bread Avenue

founded in November 2023 in the eastern part of Freetown, Bread Avenue SL is a growing bakery committed to providing freshly baked goods to our community.

Our passion for quality and tradition inspires us to use only the finest ingredients in everything we bake - from soft loaves to sweet pastries.

At bread Avenue, we don't just bake bread - we create smiles. Whether you're picking up your morning bun or placing a large order for an event, we are here to serve with love and warmth.
